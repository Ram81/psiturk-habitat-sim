Instructions

We are a group of researchers interested in building agents to do every-day tasks.

In this HIT, you will be performing a task in a 3D virtual environment using keyboard controls.

Before you perform the main task described above, you will be given a flythrough of the environment to make you acquainted with the locations. You will also be able to practice and become comfortable with the keyboard controls for navigation in the 3D house environment.

You will be prompted to finish a survey once you complete the task. If you complete the task and the survey, you will be rewarded with $x. You will receive a code for payment at the end of the study.

To summarize, you will go through four stages during this HIT:

  1. Environment flythrough
  2. Training task
  3. Actual task
  4. Survey

In total, your participation in this HIT will take about 20-25 minutes.
